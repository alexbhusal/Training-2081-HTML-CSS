Media Query

Media is a way of communication in which the information is exchanged through the use of technology. It is a medium of communication that is used to spread information, news, entertainment, advertising, etc. Media is a powerful tool that can be used to influence people and change their opinions. It can be used to educate people, entertain them, inform them, and persuade them. Media can be in the form of newspapers, magazines, television, radio, internet, social media, etc. It is an important part of our lives and plays a crucial role in shaping our society.
